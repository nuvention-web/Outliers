OUR STORY

Make R{}{}m is a private conversation space that makes it easy for you to nurture the connections you value. It could be your loved one, a new friend, a group of close friends separated by distance, or even a colleague at work. We are inspired by our personal stories, ones that we are still living through-- separated from friends and family, getting busier in our lives and struggling to Make R{}{}m for real conversations.
